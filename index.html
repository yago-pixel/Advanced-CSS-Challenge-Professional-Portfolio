Frequently Asked Questions

Is installing drywall better then plastering walls?

Drywall is so popular because installing it is considerably faster and cheaper than plastering. Because plastering involves applying a wet material, it's more time-consuming, takes longer to dry, and is more prone to cracking. It doesn't tend to be as insulating either.
